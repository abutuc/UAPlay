Do you need help?

We have joined a set of common problems, try and see if your problem is already answered.

If you can't find a movie or series using the search feature don't panic. You are most likely searching for a Title which is not currently on our data base. The reason behind this might be your location, Netflix must obey to certain regulations of streaming and therefore different countries might have different options to stream. We advise you to check if the title you're searching for is indeed availiable in your country.
If the content of the pages, like Movies and Series, are not loading and appear as almost blank then the data-base must be suffering an overload of requests. Your problem might disappear in a few minutes, however, sometimes the server might be going through maintenace. Either way, you should come back later or simply refresh the browser until the data finally appears.
Being newbies in Web Developing, in case you find any bug (visual error, data which is wrong, etc) do not hesitate on contacting us through the form bellow. You should enter your name, email and describe the bug thoroughly, providing vital information for the future debugging.
